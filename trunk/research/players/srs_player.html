Recommended Players & Technologies

  • flv.js - HTML5/MSE for playing HTTP-FLV (GitHub)
  • mpegts.js - HTML5/MSE for playing HTTP-TS (GitHub)
  • hls.js - HTML5/MSE for playing HLS (GitHub)
  • dash.js - HTML5/MSE for playing MPEG-DASH (GitHub)

Streaming Recommendations

  • For low-latency live streaming (3-5 seconds), use HTTP-FLV with flv.js player
  • For better compatibility (5-10 seconds latency), use HLS with hls.js player
  • For ultra-low latency (within 1 second), use WebRTC with RTC player
  • For audio-only streaming, use HTTP-AAC or HTTP-MP3 formats

Flash is no longer supported! Adobe Flash has reached end-of-life.
